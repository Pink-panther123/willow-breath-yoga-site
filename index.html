Our Beginner Course

Our 6-week Beginner Yoga Course is designed for complete newcomers who want a calm, supportive introduction to the practice. Over six weeks, you'll build strength, flexibility, and breath awareness through slow, guided sequences — no prior experience or flexibility required. Each class is led in a small-group setting so you get personal attention and can move at your own pace.

By the end of the course, you'll have a solid foundation in basic postures, breathing techniques, and simple routines you can continue at home.

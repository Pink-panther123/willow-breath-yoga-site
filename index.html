Our Beginner Course

Six weeks to a calmer, stronger you.

Our 6-week Beginner Yoga Course is designed for complete newcomers who want a calm, supportive introduction to the practice. Over six weeks, you'll build strength, flexibility, and breath awareness through slow, guided sequences.

By the end of the course, you'll have a solid foundation in basic postures, breathing techniques, and simple routines you can continue at home — for life.

  • ✓ No experience or flexibility required
  • ✓ Small-group setting with personal attention
  • ✓ Mats and props provided — just bring yourself
